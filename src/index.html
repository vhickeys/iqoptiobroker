WHAT IS BITCOIN Trading?

In very simple words, Bitcoin Trading is the process by which bitcoin is produced. The platform used for the production is called a Trading rig, which is a collection of different antminer machines connected together through the Blockchain network. Trading has proven to be the best way to earn load of bucks from Bitcoin. There are basically 2 types of Bitcoin Trading, namely: Hardware Trading and Cloud Trading.

  • Hardware Trading: This is the real work. Whereby a wealthy individual or an institution purchase a Bitcoin Trading Rig and perform the process of actual computing to mine bitcoin with large computing power and extreme power consumption. This type of Trading is very profitable however attracts pretty large computing power, expensive Electrical and maintenance costs.
  • Cloud Trading: Bitcoin cloud Trading unlike hardware Trading does not require large computing power or lots of electricity. Like the name implies, cloud Trading is done over the cloud. Here, the hardware miner opens up his Trading to the general public, this way an investor simply connects to the miner and begins to earn as part of the stream. Here, investors do not accrue any large maintenance or electricity costs. It is the most profitable aspect of investing in bitcoin.
